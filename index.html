What are Charms?

Only the brave or foolish explore the depths of what remains of the underground kingdom of Hollownest. Only the most seasoned adventurers make it back alive to the surface. They've learned quickly to rely on charms to increase their odds of survival.

In Hollow Knight, charms are equiptable items that grant special abilities or bonuses to the wielder. There are many charms but only a few can be equip at one time. How do you know which ones are right for you?

Here is a brief guide to help you decide which charms to use along the way on your journey, going from greenhorn to staghorn.

Beginner Charms

Great for new players but can become a crutch

In-game Cost

Thorn

When bearer takes damage, thorns sprout to damage surrounding foes

In-game Cost

Compass

Allows the bearer to locate themselves on a map

In-game Cost

Spore Shroom

When focusing SOUL, emits a spore cloud that damages surrounding foes

Exceptional Charms

Charms that are worth the cost if you have extra notches

In-game Cost

Quick Focus

Increases the speed of focusing SOUL, allows the bearer to heal quickly

In-game Cost

Longnail

Increases the range of the bearer's nail

In-game Cost

Soul Eater

Greatly increases the amount of SOUL gained from striking foes

Superior Charms

Powerful charms for all situations


In-game Cost

Quick Slash

Increases slash rate of the bearer's nail

In-game Cost

Spell Twister

Reduces the SOUL cost of casting spells

In-game Cost

Flukenest

Transforms the Vengefull Spirit spell into a horde of resilient baby flukes
